Ideal Job

As I mentioned before I have been working as a Business Analyst for a while but what I really enjoyed the most of that role was the programming part and understanding the needs for the Subject matter experts in order to create a better process for them to become more efficient and accurate and if you look at what a software developer do the first bullet point says that they typically analyse the users’ needs and then design, test and develop a software to meet those needs. If anyone wants to become a software developer they would need at least a bachelor’s degree in computer science, software engineering or a related field.

Here’s some important qualities that a software developer must have:

• Analytical skills: Developers must analyse users’ needs and then design software to meet those needs.

• Communication skills: Developers must be able to give clear instructions to others specially when working on a project. They must also explain to their customers how the software works and answer any questions that may have.

• Creativity: Developers are the creative minds behind new computer software so this is an important skill as well.

• Detail oriented: Developers often work on many parts of an application or system at the same time and must therefore be able to concentrate and pay attention to detail.

• Interpersonal skills: Software developers must be able to work well with others who contribute to designing, developing, and programming successful software.

• Problem-solving skills: Because developers are in charge of software from beginning to end, they must be able to solve problems that arise throughout the design process.
